WHERE IT STARTED

The organization was first started in the year 2023, by Chef Christian Obosi who had an inspired goal to help chefs all over the world learn new recipes and even create new ones from their shared experiences.

BossRecipe has not only created a large diverse platform for experienced chefs to interact but also sustained new relationships with beginners, helping them and nurturing them to become the best of the best.

WHAT WE OFFER

BossRecipe gives chefs the opportunity to share their recipes with other chefs. It also offers chefs the chance to learn other dishes and also create absolutely new dishes.
